Jerome Lopez

Jerome is a character introduced to the BCU in 2021. His original game is Dark Souls Remastered. He is best known for his giant nose and strength based weapons. He can be found in many games including Monster Hunter World, and Grand Theft Auto V. He was designed and played by Roman.

Character History

Jerome Lopez was created by Roman in 2021. He is best known for his giant nose and strength based weaponry. Jerome was originally created in Dark Souls Remastered and had a very skinny face and a giant great club as his weapon. In later iterations of Jerome, his face evened out more and he used other giant weapons such as the great sword from Monster Hunter: World and Prelate's Inferno Crozier from Elden Ring. Jerome has killed the hardest boss in his specified game but is not max level.

Jerome appears in multiple titles such as Monster Hunter: World, Elden Ring, Grand Theft Auto V, and The Sims 4. Jeromes appearance in Elden ring is similar to his Dark Souls Remastered appearance, but his face is slightly more fleshed out. In Grand Theft Auto V, we see a younger version of Jerome in his early 20's roaming the streets of Los Santos. In Monster Hunter: World, Jerome sports a long sword or a great sword and wears heavy armor. Jerome's appearance in The Sims 4 is different then all the rest. He has a wife and kids and is a fitness teacher. His wifes name is Stephanie Lopez.
