Allie

Allie is a character introduced to the BCU in 2020. Her original game is Monster Hunter: World. She is known for her signature white hair and white clothes, and long sword at her back. She can be found in many games including Monster Hunter Rise, and Grand Theft Auto V. She was designed and played by Roman.

Character History

Allie was created in 2020 by Roman. Nowadays, she is known for her signature white outfit and flowing white hair. Originally, she was designed with short pink hair, and she wore armor. She originally used two daggers as her main weapon but has since started using a long sword as her primary. Allie has killed the hardest monster in her specified game but is not max level.

Allie appears in multiple games including Monster Hunter Rise, Grand Theft Auto V, and The Sims 4. In GTAV she sports her signature white hair, and a beany. In Monster Hunter Rise she wears a fox mask.
